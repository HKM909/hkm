Seasonal Cuisines of India

Summer: Mangoes Lassi

Summer in India is synonymous with mangoes.
Dishes like mango chutney and
mango lassi (a yogurt-based drink) are popular during this season.

Monsoon: Pakoras and Chai

During the monsoon, hot and crispy
pakoras (fritters) paired with steaming masala
chai (tea) are a favorite snack.

Winter: Sarson da Saag and Makki di Roti

In winter, the combination
of sarson da saag (mustard greens)
and makki di roti (corn flatbread) is a hearty and warming meal.

Festive Season: Sweets and Snacks

During festivals, a variety
of sweets like ladoos, barfis, and
snacks are made to celebrate, with each region having its specialties.
